# User Interests

I am deeply interested in AI and machine learning — especially large language
models, reasoning systems, alignment research, and how these technologies are
being deployed in production systems.

**High priority topics:**
- LLM architecture advances (new models, training techniques, scaling laws)
- AI safety and alignment research
- Real-world AI deployment: agents, RAG systems, fine-tuning
- AI policy, regulation, and its societal impact
- Notable research papers with practical implications

**Also interested in:**
- Software engineering best practices and system design
- Developer tools, productivity, and open-source ecosystems
- Startup news in the AI space

**What to skip:**
- Vague corporate press releases with no technical substance
- Hype-driven announcements without concrete details
- Anything unrelated to technology, AI, or engineering
